About Me

I'm currently a student studying Health Informatics at the University of Waterloo, and an MLT. Prior to that, I spent for 3 years as a genetics tech as part of an amazing team doing genetic testing at the Princess Margaret Cancer Center. Since then, I've taken an interest in the healthcare system more generally and hope play a role in improving it in the years to come. That being said, my expertise is largely inline with my interests: medical lab genetics, genome and cancer informatics, cancer biology, health systems and biomedical data analysis.

I'm currently in the process of learning web development and picking up data science skills.

Philosophy of Life / Motivation

As far as what motivates me it's essentially this: We are all members of spaceship Earth and we all have role to play in the future of humanity in the Universe. For some, life is over when death takes us. But, the way I think of it we are all one and we all contribute to and are a part of the collective whole, humanity. There is no difference between me and the next person, and we all must contribute to one another's well being so as to maximize our civilization's ability to progress. To that end, it's love, truth, beauty, and progress towards a better future which serve as my main motivators in life.

Aside from that I like to think I'm a pretty friendly, open-minded person and I like meeting people and enjoy having discussions. If your curious about me, what I do, or are interested in working on something with me feel free to get in touch!
